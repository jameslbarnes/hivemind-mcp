What are OAuth Credentials?

OAuth credentials allow Claude Desktop to securely access your Scribe account through the MCP connector. They consist of:

  • Client ID: A public identifier for your connection
  • Client Secret: A private key that authenticates your connection

Once generated, you'll add these to Claude Desktop's connector settings to establish the connection.
